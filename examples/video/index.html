Tips for accessible Autoplay

  1. If possible avoid autoplaying
  2. If possible avoid looping
  3. If you must autoplay, play it less than 5 seconds and then pause
  4. Overlay a pause/play button on top of the background video may be in top or bottom right
  5. Make sure hitting the ‘Esc’ key will stop it playing.
  6. Avoid combining audio with animations unless absolutely necessary.
  7. Disable it alltogether for people who have Reduce motion preference enabled
