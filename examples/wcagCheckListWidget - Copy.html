WCAG 2 Checklist

Checkpoint Summary Descrition Comments
1.1.1 Non-text Content Provide text alternatives for any non-text content
  1. Images, image buttons, and image map hot spots have appropriate, equivalent alternative text.
  2. Images that do not convey content, are decorative, or contain content that is already conveyed in text
  3. are given empty alternative text (alt="") or implemented as CSS backgrounds. All linked images have descriptive alternative text.
  4. Equivalent alternatives to complex images are provided in context or on a separate linked page.
  5. Form buttons have a descriptive value.
  6. Inputs have associated accessible names.
  7. Embedded multimedia is identified via accessible text.
  8. Frames and iframes are appropriately titled.
